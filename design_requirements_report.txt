LASERCOM POINTING CONTROL DESIGN REQUIREMENTS
=======================================================

----------------------------------------
  bandwidth: 5.0 Hz ± 0.5
    Coarse pointing bandwidth for large angle maneuvers
  settling_time: 0.2 s ± 0.05
    Settling time for 1° step input
  overshoot: 10.0 % ± 5.0
    Maximum overshoot for step response
  phase_margin: 45.0 deg ± 10.0
    Phase margin for stability

FSM FINE CONTROL
----------------------------------------
  bandwidth: 200.0 Hz ± 20.0
    Fine pointing bandwidth for residual error correction
  steady_state_error: 1e-06 rad ± 1e-07
    Steady-state pointing accuracy
  settling_time: 0.01 s ± 0.005
    Settling time for fine corrections
